About Me

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, living in the city's rich and culturually diverse east side. It was early on I developed a love for engineering and tinkering, often spending days working on figuring out how things worked, both mechanically and electronically. Eventually, I earned my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UC San Diego. I worked professionally as an aerospace engineer designing and manufacturing fluid components for satellite and spacecraft propulsion systems. As my career progressed, I gained many skills but my enjoyment of tinkering and problem never faded. It was around this time, I decided to pick up Ruby and JavaScript and I knew I made the right decision when I create my first web application out of pure passion. The overwhelming joy from creating something you love with code was exhilirating. I'm continually updating my skillset and am excited to see what I can build in the future!
